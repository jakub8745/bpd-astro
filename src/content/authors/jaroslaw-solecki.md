Jarosław (Jarek) Solecki is an artist living in England and a graduate of the University of Arts in Poznań (Poland). He is also the winner of an Arts Council England art grant. He is currently a research fellow at the Institute of European Culture of the Polish University Abroad in London (PUNO), where he is leading research in the anthropology of visuality and new media. 
#### He specialises:
in sculpture, performance art in public space (AR/XR), socially engaged art (social exclusion) and the possibilities of combining new technologies and art with visual communication, code art. He also took part in the art and research project TICASS: Imaging technologies in communication, art and social sciences (Horizon 2020 EU no 734602). His latest project is the Virtual Archive of the Blue Point Art Gallery London, which merges new technologies with art and the humanities.
